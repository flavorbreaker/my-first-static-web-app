Averett University

Computer Science and Information Systems

About: The university offers Computer Science (CS) and Information Systems (IS) majors, as well as a selection of minors. The IS program can be completed entirely through Averett Online. The programs teach a variety of skills including:

  • Computing theory
  • Computer programming and programming languages
  • Systems design and administration
  • Database design and administration
  • Computer project management
  • Software programming
